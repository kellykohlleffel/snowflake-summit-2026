## Abstract (Option A — Strategic/Bold)

**Title: From Connectors to Data Agents in 20 Minutes**

What if you could go from a source database to an AI agent answering business questions — with insights flowing back to your application — in 20 minutes? In this hands-on lab, you'll experience Fivetran's complete data lifecycle powered by Snowflake Cortex Code. Starting with a retail dataset in Google Cloud PostgreSQL, Fivetran extracts and loads data through its Managed Data Lake Service to S3 in Apache Iceberg format, automatically cataloged in Snowflake Horizon Catalog. Fivetran dbt Transformations build analytics-ready models through bronze, silver, and gold layers. A Snowflake Semantic View defines what the numbers mean, and a Cortex Agent brings it all to life — answering business questions in natural language. Finally, Fivetran Activation pushes insights back to a live business application, closing the loop. Move, Manage, Transform, Activate — Fivetran's end-to-end data lifecycle meets Snowflake's AI platform. This is Open Data Infrastructure in action. Walk up, sit down, and see the future of data engineering.

**Level:** All levels (data engineers, architects, executives, analysts)
**Duration:** 20 minutes
**Format:** Instructor-led, walk-up, 6 pre-configured laptops provided

## Abstract (Option B — Technical/Specific)

**Title: From Connectors to Data Agents in 20 Minutes with Fivetran and Cortex Code**

Experience the complete Fivetran + Snowflake data lifecycle in a 20-minute hands-on lab — entirely within Snowflake Cortex Code. Starting with a retail dataset in Google Cloud PostgreSQL, Fivetran extracts and loads data through its Managed Data Lake Service (MDLS) to S3 in Apache Iceberg format, where it appears automatically in Snowflake Horizon Catalog — ready for Snowflake compute. A Snowflake Semantic View defines the business metrics and dimensions, and a Cortex Agent powered by Cortex Analyst brings the data to life — answering business questions in natural language. Finally, Fivetran Activation closes the loop by pushing insights back to a live business application. Six steps, from source database to AI-powered intelligence and back into action. See how Fivetran's Move, Manage, Transform, and Activate capabilities combine with Snowflake's AI platform to turn raw data into business outcomes.

**Level:** All levels
**Duration:** 20 minutes
**Format:** Instructor-led, walk-up, 6 pre-configured laptops provided

## Abstract (Option C — Curiosity/Outcome-Driven)

**Title: Build a Complete AI Data Pipeline — Without Opening a Single UI**

No Fivetran dashboard. No Snowflake worksheet. No code editor. Just Snowflake Cortex Code. In this 20-minute hands-on lab, you'll experience a production-quality data pipeline built from scratch — from a PostgreSQL source through Fivetran MDLS to S3 in open Iceberg format, transformed with Fivetran dbt Transformations, brought to life by a Snowflake Cortex Agent that answers business questions in natural language, and activated back to a live business application via Fivetran Activation. You'll see Fivetran's complete data lifecycle — Move, Manage, Transform, Activate — working together to make data AI-ready and deliver intelligence back to where teams work. Whether you're a data engineer, an executive, or just curious about the future of data pipelines, you'll walk away understanding what's possible when Fivetran and Snowflake's AI platform work together.

**Level:** All levels — no technical prerequisites
**Duration:** 20 minutes
**Format:** Instructor-led, walk-up, 6 pre-configured laptops provided
